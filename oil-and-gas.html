The Oil and Gas Sector

Introduction to Petroleum Engineering

The oil and gas industry is a cornerstone of the modern energy sector, providing fuel for transportation, power generation, and raw materials for chemicals and plastics. Petroleum engineering is at the heart of exploring, extracting, and refining crude oil and natural gas.

Exploration and Extraction Techniques

Discover the cutting-edge techniques used in the search for underground or underwater oil and gas fields, and learn about the drilling methods employed to extract these valuable resources.

Refinement and Distribution

Refining crude oil into usable products is a complex process. Understand how chemical engineers contribute to the refinement process and the distribution of petroleum products across the globe.
