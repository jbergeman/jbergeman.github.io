Biotechnology and Chemical Engineering

The Intersection of Biology and Engineering

Biotechnology merges biological systems with engineering principles, leading to the creation of products that can improve health, fight diseases, and reduce environmental impact.

Genetic Engineering and Bioprocessing

Dive into the world of genetic engineering, where chemical engineers manipulate genetic material to enhance or create new functions in organisms, leading to breakthroughs in medicine, agriculture, and biofuels.

Environmental Biotechnology

Discover how chemical engineers use biotechnology to address environmental challenges, from developing bio-based materials to bioremediation techniques that clean up contaminated environments.
